Frequently Asked Questions

I’ve tried everything – tablets, therapy, even hypnosis. Why would a patch work?

I completely understand your scepticism. The difference is: BioEdge™ doesn’t fight your craving – it calms the overstimulated nervous system that triggers the craving in the first place. The transdermal delivery bypasses your stomach (no nausea) and gives you 24 hours of steady support. Many of our customers say: “It’s the first time I didn’t have to fight.”

What if my family finds out? I’m so ashamed...

The patch is completely discreet – flat like a normal plaster, skin-toned, invisible under normal clothing. You can wear it on the upper arm, shoulder blade or even the thigh. No one has to know until you’re ready to share your success story.

Will I get withdrawal symptoms? I’m afraid of seizures...

BioEdge™ is NOT a substitute for medical care in severe alcoholism. But: the ingredients (especially magnesium and B-vitamins) are specifically dosed to stabilise your nervous system and ease withdrawal symptoms. 89% of our users report a gentle transition without the dreaded symptoms. If in doubt: speak to your doctor – many recommend BioEdge™ as supportive therapy.

How long do I need to wear it before I’m truly free?

Most feel the first changes after 3–7 days – better sleep, less shaking, a calmer mind. For lasting freedom, we recommend at least 3 months. Remember: it took years to get here – give yourself time for real healing. The good thing is: with each day it gets easier, not harder.

What if I relapse? Does that make me a hopeless case?

No, never! Healing isn’t a straight line. If you have a bad day, simply put on a new patch the next morning. No reset to zero, no shame, no “failure”. BioEdge™ is your quiet companion, always there for you – without judgement.
