Frequently Asked Questions
Do CocoGlam patches contain hormones or estrogen?
No. Our patches are completely hormone-free and contain no estrogen. They work by delivering natural botanical ingredients through the skin they do not directly interfere with your hormones or endocrine system in any way.
Can CocoGlam patches cause breast cancer?
Absolutely not. There are no ingredients in our patches that have any link to breast cancer. They do not contain hormones, and they are formulated with safe, plant-based actives designed for external use only.
Will the patches work if I have breast implants?
That depends on how your implants were placed. If your implants were positioned under your natural breast tissue, Cherry Glam patches can still enhance the appearance of your natural tissue over time. If your implants were placed over the tissue, the patches will not be effective because they work on your natural breast tissue.
Is this safe for sensitive skin?
Yes! CocoGlam patches are made with gentle botanical ingredients and medical-grade adhesives. We formulated them to be kind to sensitive skin. If you have a history of adhesive sensitivity, we always recommend doing a small patch test first.
How soon will I see results?
Many women report feeling subtle changes (like smoother, firmer skin) within the first 34 weeks. Visible enhancement typically builds gradually over 612 weeks with consistent use. Every body is different, so we encourage sticking with the full routine for best results.
What if I stop using the patches? Will I lose my results?
Like any beauty or self-care routine, ongoing use helps maintain your results. If you stop using CocoGlam patches completely, your skin and tissue will gradually return to their natural baseline over time.
How many patches come in each pack, and how often should I use them?
Each pack of CocoGlam patches contains 4 patches (2 pairs) enough for two full applications (one pair per use). We recommend using one pair of patches once per week as part of your regular routine. However, many women choose to apply them multiple times per week (23 times) to help accelerate and enhance results. The patches are designed for single use for best performance, apply fresh patches each time.
