Does it contain bee-derived ingredients?

Yes. Gum Therapy includes bee venom. Do not use if you’re allergic to bees or bee products. If unsure, consult your dentist or healthcare professional before use.

Can I use it with my current routine?

Yes. Brush with Gum Therapy twice daily. You can continue flossing and using a soft brush. If you use additional treatments, ask your dentist how best to combine them.

When might I notice less sensitivity?

Everyone’s mouth is different. Many people feel improvements as consistent brushing becomes routine. Keep using as directed and follow professional dental care for best outcomes.

Is it suitable for sensitive mouths?

It’s designed to be gentle. If irritation occurs, stop use and consult your dentist. Avoid swallowing and keep out of reach of children.
