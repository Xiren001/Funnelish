BioEdge is formulated with a rich blend of ingredients that are mostly plant-based and entirely vegan. Our commitment to using vegan, plant-based ingredients ensures our serum is not only effective but also ethical and sustainable.

Syn-Ake® is completely safe. It does not contain real venom; it is a lab-created peptide clinically tested for topical skincare. It is non-toxic, non-irritating, and suitable for daily use, making it an effective and safe choice for women seeking smoother, firmer skin without invasive treatments.

These ingredients work together to hydrate, firm, and smooth your skin. They promote collagen production, accelerate cell turnover, deeply moisturize, and help relax the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles for a younger-looking complexion.
