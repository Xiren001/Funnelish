How exactly do you use the Glow Remedy Oil?

Cleanse your skin with warm water and a gentle cleanser. Apply a few drops of Glow Remedy Oil to your fingertips and gently massage onto face, neck, décolletage, and any other areas with sagging skin or wrinkles using upward motions. For best results, use morning and evening daily, focusing on problem areas like upper arms, abdomen, and thighs. Allow to fully absorb before applying makeup or other skincare products.
